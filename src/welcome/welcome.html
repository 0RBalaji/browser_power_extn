Preserve Media

Readable Text

Easy Controls

Works Everywhere

Compatible with Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Brave browsers.

Getting Started

  1. Click the Dark Browser icon in your toolbar
  2. Toggle dark mode on or off
  3. Exclude websites you don't want darkened
  4. Enjoy comfortable browsing!

💡 Tips

  • Use the popup to exclude specific websites
  • Visit settings to choose different color themes
  • Dark mode works on dynamically loaded content too
