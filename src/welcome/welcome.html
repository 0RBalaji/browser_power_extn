Dark Browser

Bright pages become easy on the eyes—without wrecking photos, video, or layout.

What you get

  • Core

    Site-wide darkening

    Flips light UIs to comfortable dark tones as pages load and update.

  • Media

    Images & video stay natural

    Preserves media and embedded content so nothing looks inverted or wrong.

  • Control

    Per-site rules

    Exclude domains when a site already looks right or breaks when themed.

  • Look

    Themes

    Pick a palette in the popup—Default, AMOLED, or Blue—to match your taste.

Start in three steps

  1. 1

    Open the popup

    Click the Dark Browser icon in the toolbar.

  2. 2

    Toggle & tune

    Turn dark mode on or off and choose a theme.

  3. 3

    Exclude when needed

    Add exceptions for sites that should stay as-is.
